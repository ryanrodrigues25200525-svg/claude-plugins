# /compounders

Find high-quality long-term compounders with durable economics.

## Tools
- edgartools — multi-year financial history
- openbb-mcp — quality metrics and valuations

## Compounder Criteria
- ROIC consistently >15%
- Revenue growth 10%+ annually for 5+ years
- Expanding or stable margins
- Strong FCF conversion (>80% of net income)
- Net cash or manageable debt
- Durable competitive advantage (moat)
- Management with long tenure and ownership

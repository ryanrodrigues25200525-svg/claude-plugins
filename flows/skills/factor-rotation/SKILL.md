# /factor-rotation

Identify which factors are outperforming — value, growth, quality, momentum, low vol.

## Tools
- openbb-mcp — equity_price_performance, etf_price_performance (factor ETFs)

## Factor ETF Proxies
- Value: VTV, IVE
- Growth: VUG, IVW
- Quality: QUAL, DGRW
- Momentum: MTUM, PDP
- Low Vol: USMV, SPLV
- Small Cap: IWM, IJR

# /leadership-watch

Identify stocks and sectors leading the market.

## Tools
- openbb-mcp — equity_discovery_gainers, equity_price_performance, equity_discovery_active

## Output Format
```
Leadership Watch
[Checked: timestamp]

MARKET LEADERS (strongest relative strength)
| Ticker | 1M RS | 3M RS | Sector | Theme |
|--------|-------|-------|--------|-------|

SECTOR LEADERS
- #1: [Sector] — [Why it's leading]
- #2: [Sector] — [Why it's leading]
- #3: [Sector] — [Why it's leading]

EMERGING LEADERSHIP (breaking out, new leaders)
| Ticker | Breakout level | Volume | Theme |

FADING LEADERSHIP (former leaders weakening)
| Ticker | Signal | Risk |

MARKET THEME FROM LEADERSHIP
[What the leading stocks tell us about the macro/market narrative]

ROTATION SIGNAL
[Are we rotating from one leadership group to another?]
```

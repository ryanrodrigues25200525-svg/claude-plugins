# /research TOPIC

Generate institutional-quality research report.

## Tools
- paper-search-mcp — academic literature
- openbb-mcp — market data and economic data
- google-news — recent developments

## Execution
1. Search academic literature for foundational research on TOPIC
2. Pull relevant market/economic data
3. Pull recent news and developments
4. Synthesise into structured research report

## Output Format
```
Research Report — TOPIC
[Checked: timestamp]

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
[3-4 sentences: key finding and recommendation]

BACKGROUND
[Context and why this topic matters now]

ACADEMIC EVIDENCE
[Key papers and findings]

CURRENT DATA
[Relevant market/economic data]

RECENT DEVELOPMENTS
[Last 30-90 days of relevant news]

ANALYSIS
[Synthesis of academic + data + news]

INVESTMENT IMPLICATIONS
- [Specific asset classes, sectors, or trades]
- Bull case: [Upside scenario]
- Bear case: [Downside scenario]

CONCLUSION
[Final view and confidence level]

SOURCES: [Academic papers, data sources, news]
```

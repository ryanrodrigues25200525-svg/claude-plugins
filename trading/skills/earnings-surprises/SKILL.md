# /earnings-surprises

Find recent earnings beats and misses across the market.

## Tools
- edgartools — recent filings
- google-news — earnings reactions

## Execution
1. Search for earnings reports filed in the last 2 weeks
2. Compare actuals vs estimates where available
3. Identify largest beats and misses
4. Analyze post-earnings price reactions

## Output Format
```
Earnings Surprises — Recent Results
[Checked: timestamp]

BIGGEST BEATS
| Ticker | EPS Beat % | Revenue Beat % | Stock Reaction | Sector |
|--------|------------|----------------|----------------|--------|

BIGGEST MISSES
| Ticker | EPS Miss % | Revenue Miss % | Stock Reaction | Sector |

OPPORTUNITIES
- Beaten down on good results (buy the dip): [Tickers]
- Rallied on bad results (short candidate): [Tickers]

SECTOR TRENDS: [Which sectors are beating/missing consistently]
```

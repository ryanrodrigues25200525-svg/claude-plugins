# /spin-off-watch

Monitor announced and pending corporate spin-offs.

## Tools
- edgartools — Form 10, S-11, 8-K spin-off filings

## Execution
1. Search edgartools for spin-off related filings
2. Search news for spin-off announcements
3. Track status and expected timelines

## Output Format
```
Spin-off Watch
[Checked: timestamp]

PENDING SPIN-OFFS
| Parent | SpinCo | Business | Expected Date | Rationale |
|--------|--------|----------|---------------|-----------|

RECENTLY COMPLETED
| Parent | SpinCo | Date | Performance Since |

RUMOURED
| Company | Potential SpinCo | Source |

OPPORTUNITY ASSESSMENT
- [Which spin-offs represent the best investment opportunity]
- [Classic spin-off dynamics: parent selling pressure, SpinCo undervalued]
```

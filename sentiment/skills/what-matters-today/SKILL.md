# /what-matters-today

Daily market briefing — most important developments across all markets.

## Tools
- google-news — top financial news
- fed-speech-mcp — any Fed communications today
- openbb-mcp — economic calendar, market data

## Execution
1. Pull today's economic releases and results
2. Pull top market-moving news
3. Check for Fed speeches or central bank events
4. Check earnings releases today
5. Synthesise into priority-ordered briefing

## Output Format
```
What Matters Today — [Date]
[Checked: timestamp]

🔴 MUST KNOW (market moving)
- [Event/news + market impact]

🟡 WATCH (developing situation)
- [Event/news + potential impact]

📊 DATA TODAY
- [Economic releases, actual vs expected]

💬 FED / CENTRAL BANKS
- [Any speeches or policy communications]

📈 EARNINGS TODAY
- [Companies reporting + key numbers]

🌍 OVERNIGHT / INTERNATIONAL
- [Key developments from Asia/Europe]

MARKET SETUP
- S&P futures: X | Nasdaq: X | Risk: On/Off
- Key thing to watch today: [Single most important focus]
```

# /market-narratives

Identify the dominant themes driving markets right now.

## Tools
- google-news — financial media themes
- fed-speech-mcp — policy narrative

## Execution
1. Scan major financial news for recurring themes
2. Pull Fed communication for policy narrative
3. Identify consensus views and contrarian opportunities

## Output Format
```
Market Narratives
[Checked: timestamp]

DOMINANT NARRATIVES (what everyone is talking about)
1. [Narrative]: [Evidence, who believes it, market impact]
2. [Narrative]: [Evidence, who believes it, market impact]
3. [Narrative]: [Evidence, who believes it, market impact]

FADING NARRATIVES (losing market attention)
- [Narrative that was consensus but is shifting]

EMERGING NARRATIVES (early stage, not yet consensus)
- [New theme developing]

CONTRARIAN TAKE
[What is the market getting wrong? What narrative is priced in that shouldn't be?]

POSITIONING IMPLICATION
[How narratives translate to positioning in equities, bonds, FX, commodities]
```

# /country-report COUNTRY

Institutional-style macroeconomic country report.

## Tools
- openbb-mcp — full economy suite
- eurostat-mcp — EU specific data
- google-news — recent developments, political risk

## Execution
1. Pull all economic indicators
2. Pull recent political and economic news
3. Synthesize into institutional research format

## Output Format
```
COUNTRY — Macroeconomic Report
[Checked: timestamp]

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
[3-4 sentences: overall assessment]

GROWTH OUTLOOK
- Current momentum: [analysis]
- Leading indicators: [PMI, business confidence]
- Forecast: [growth trajectory]

INFLATION & MONETARY POLICY
- Inflation trend and drivers
- Central bank stance and rate path
- Risks to inflation outlook

LABOR MARKET
- Employment conditions
- Wage dynamics
- Structural factors

FISCAL POSITION
- Deficit trajectory
- Debt sustainability
- Political constraints on fiscal policy

EXTERNAL SECTOR
- Trade dynamics, FX, current account
- Export composition and vulnerabilities

POLITICAL & STRUCTURAL RISKS
- [Key political risks]
- [Structural weaknesses]

INVESTMENT IMPLICATIONS
- Currency: [Bullish/Bearish]
- Bonds: [Bullish/Bearish]
- Equities: [Bullish/Bearish]
- Key trades: [Specific opportunities]
```

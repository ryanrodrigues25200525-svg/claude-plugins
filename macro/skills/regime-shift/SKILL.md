# /regime-shift

Detect major transitions in economic conditions before they become consensus.

## Tools
- openbb-mcp — leading indicators, yield curve, PMI, credit spreads
- fed-speech-mcp — policy signals
- eurostat-mcp — EU indicators

## Execution
1. Assess current macro regime
2. Look for early signals of regime transition
3. Identify what would confirm or deny the shift
4. Map to portfolio implications

## Output Format
```
Regime Shift Monitor
[Checked: timestamp]

CURRENT REGIME: [Goldilocks / Reflation / Stagflation / Slowdown / Recession]
Time in regime: ~X months

EARLY TRANSITION SIGNALS
🔴 Strong signal of shift: [Indicator, reading, why it matters]
🟡 Watch: [Indicators approaching inflection]
🟢 Contradicts shift: [Counter-evidence]

MOST LIKELY TRANSITION
From: [Current] → To: [Next regime]
Probability: X%
Timeline: X-X months
Trigger: [What event/data confirms the shift]

WHAT TO WATCH
- [Key data releases that would confirm/deny]
- [Market signals to monitor: spreads, curve, commodities]

PORTFOLIO PLAYBOOK IF REGIME SHIFTS
- Rotate into: [Asset classes, sectors]
- Reduce: [What to trim]
- Hedge with: [Instruments]
```
